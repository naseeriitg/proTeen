The Origin Story

Born in the Wular Valley, Kashmir

In the orchards surrounding Wular Lake — Asia's largest freshwater lake — Kagzi walnut trees have grown for over a century. Hand-tended by farmers who have never used chemical fertilisers. Harvested in September when the valley turns gold.

Most Kashmir walnuts leave the valley as raw commodity. proTeen was built to change that. We go directly to the farmers. We know their names. We have stood in their orchards.

Every proTeen bar contains 60g of Kagzi kernel — the finest thin-shelled variety — delivering 5.4g of plant Omega-3 ALA. More than any other nut. More than any other bar.
